Package Cost Inclusions

  • 1 : Meals: Daily Breakfast (except on the arrival day).
  • 2 : Accommodation on Twin or Double share Basis.
  • 3 : Package includes all transfers and sightseeing by AC vehicle in Havelock and a Non AC vehicle in Port Blair/Neil.
  • 4 : Entrance fees, and Ferry Tickets to all Sightseeing Places as per itinerary; road taxes, parking fee,Driver allowances, fuel charges, interstate taxes and portage at all hotels.
  • 5 : Services of a Tour Manager at Port Blair,Neil and Havelock.
  • 6 : Separate Vehicle in Port Blair and Havelock.
  • 7 : Port Blair-Havelock-Port Blair by Pvt. Cruise Boat.

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • Day : 1 Port Blair – City Tour (150 Km – 4 hrs)

    Upon Arrival at Port Blair Airport,Pick up from airport and drop hotel, after lunch trip to Anthropological museum,Historical Cellular Jai,Fisheris museum,Chatam saw mill, and evening Light & Sound show in Cellular Jail. Return to hotel.

  • Day : 2 Port Blair – Neil Island

    Early Morning after breakfast, leave for Neil Island in Pvt cruise trip to Sitapur,Laxmanpur and Bharatpur beach.Evening return to resort.

  • Day : 3 Neil Island - Havelock Island – Sightseeing

    After breakfast , leave for Havelock Island in Pvt cruise, trip to Radhanagar beach.Evening return to resort.

  • Day : 4 Havelock Island – Port Blair (2 hrs 67kms)

    After breakfast, trip to Elephant beach where 1 session of snorkeling for all members is included in the package, Evening drop to resort.

  • Day : 5 Port Blair – Sightseeing

    After breakfast, check out from resort leave for PB in by Pvt. cruise boat reach Port Blair pick up from jetty, one can have a shopping session in port blair and check in at hotel.

  • Day : 6 Departure Transfer till Airport

    As as per your Flight schedule you will be transferred to Port Blair Airport.We await for your next visit. Bon Voyage. Service ends.
